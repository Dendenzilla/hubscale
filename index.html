About Us

We are striving to make performance marketing better

We were once UA Managers. Today, we are UA Architects, united by a common goal to change the way things are done and to never stop inventing. As the story goes, we were four UA enthusiasts who gradually grew tired of the reality of our industry: fragmented workflows, retroactive optimization with weeklong lags (we called them traffic postmortems), data scattered over a gazillion platforms, and manual labor unworthy of humans. We had to rethink it, and with this goal on our fridge and in our hearts, we set out to build Hubscale: a 360 platform to end all platforms and alleviate all our aching pain points.

We’re already working with gaming companies, e-commerce apps, and agencies which are reporting significant increases in ROI and work capacity per UA Manager, making our effort worth-while. So if you want to see the Hubscale system and explore our unique solution, hit the Demo button and let’s get to work.
